# Nuerra

Nuerra is a Nintendocore supergroup, made up of [Unicorn Hole](/wiki/Unicorn_Hole "Unicorn Hole"), [Polygon Horizon](/wiki/Polygon_Horizon "Polygon Horizon"), and [Starcutter](/wiki/Starcutter?action=edit&redlink=1 "Starcutter (page does not exist)").

## History / NCU Involvement

The group was spearheaded by [Uhole](/wiki/UnicornHole?action=edit&redlink=1 "UnicornHole (page does not exist)") to create chapter 3 of the NCU, [Scars](/wiki/Scars "Scars"). This album focuses on [Isaac](/wiki/Isaac "Isaac") and [Sloan](/wiki/Sloan "Sloan"), as they track down Grand Demon [Silver](/wiki/Silver "Silver"). Nuerra has released several singles since Scars.

[![[images/200px-Scars_-_Main_Icon.png|Image]]](/wiki/File:Scars_-_Main_Icon.png)"Scars" is Nuerra's only full length release so far
